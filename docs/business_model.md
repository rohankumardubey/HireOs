# Business Model

## Target customers

- Startup recruiting teams
- Growth-stage talent operations
- Enterprise HR and recruiting organizations
- Agencies that need structured screening but human-controlled decisions

## Pain points

- Too much manual resume screening
- Inconsistent interviewer quality
- Weak visibility into why candidates were advanced or rejected
- Poor analytics around AI-assisted hiring workflows

## Value proposition

HireOS AI is not just an AI interviewer. It is a hiring intelligence platform with explainable scoring, recruiter control, analytics, and AI evaluation infrastructure.

## Pricing tiers

- Free trial
- Startup
- Growth
- Enterprise

## Moat

- Recruiter-centered workflow design
- Explainable scoring plus override capture
- Analytics and model-quality instrumentation
- Event-driven architecture that scales into internal hiring intelligence

## Competitors

- point solutions for async interviewing
- ATS plugins with shallow AI features
- generic chatbot interview tools

## Differentiation

- Stronger human-in-the-loop posture
- Better auditability and score explanation
- More data-platform-aware architecture
- Demo-friendly and enterprise-extensible from the same codebase

## Enterprise features

- SSO
- private deployment
- custom scoring rubrics
- ATS integrations
- role-specific compliance controls

## Data privacy positioning

- Avoids protected attributes in scoring
- Keeps recruiters in the final-decision loop
- Stores audit trails and explanations for governance reviews

## Go-to-market ideas

- Talent ops demo motion for scaling startups
- ATS-adjacent pilot deployments
- compliance-first positioning for enterprise HR leaders
- engineering-led growth through transparent architecture and evaluation maturity
